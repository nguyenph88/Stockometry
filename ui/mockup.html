How Stockometry Works

1

Historical Trend Analysis

The bot analyzes news from the last 6 days to establish a baseline sentiment trend for each market sector. This helps identify the underlying "market mood."

2

Daily Impact Analysis

It then scans only today's news for specific, high-impact events like new regulations, corporate deals, or major announcements that could act as a short-term catalyst.

3

High-Confidence Signal Generation

A high-confidence signal is generated only when a daily impact event **confirms** an existing historical trend. This confluence of evidence is what powers our most actionable insights.
